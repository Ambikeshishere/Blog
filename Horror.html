Hi everyone I am Ambikesh Srivastava, I study in Prayagraj and this is story from my college life......

It was supposed to be the time of my life. I was finally a college student, away from my parents, free to do whatever I pleased. But as it turned out, being a student was nothing like I had imagined it to be.

It all began on my first night in the hostel room. I heard strange noises coming from the walls. It was as if someone was scratching on the walls, trying to get in. I tried to ignore it and go to sleep, but I couldn't shake off the fuzzy feeling that someone or something was in the room with me.

As the weeks went by, things only got worse. My grades started to slip and I found myself constantly feeling scared and depressed. I would hear footsteps in the hallway when no one was there, and my belongings started going missing.

One day, I was studying in the library when I saw a figure in the corner of my eye. It was a girl dressed in all white, with long hair that covered her face. I tried to ignore her, but she kept staring at me with an expressionless face. I decided to leave, but as I walked out, I felt someone grab my arm. It was the girl in white, and her face was now twisted into an evil smile.

From that point on, things escalated quickly. I would see her everywhere I went, and the scratching on the walls in my room became louder and more persistent. It was as if she was trying to break through the walls and into my room.

One night, I woke up to find her standing at the foot of my bed, staring at me with that same evil grin. She started moving closer and closer, until I could feel her breath on my face. I tried to scream, but my voice seemed to have left me.

That was the last thing I remembered before waking up. My friends told me that I had been slept in library, and then I quickly packed bag and I return to my Hometown. I never saw that girl again but I tell this to my mom, she said to change the hostel room or either I try a PG. I came to college and start a new journey but sometimes I felt there is something behind me……………
