Hi Freinds, mera naam Abhay hai aur mai isee saal engineering college me admission liya hu. Us din ki ek choti si story aapko sunana chahta hu.First of all, mai aapko apne baare me kuch batata hu mai U.P. ke ek jile se Prayagraj (dil se Allahabad ) se belong karta hu. Mai bhi sabhi ki tarah 10 ke baad math le li aur lag gya IIT ki taiyaari me. As usual mera IIT me to kya IIIT me bhi nhi hua to maine ek college me admission le liya . Ab college kaisa hi ho kaise bhi mila ho, excitement to rahti hai. Ab wo din aa gya tha jab mujhe pahle din college jana tha.Pahli baar mujhe college jaane me excitement ho rhi thi kyuki covid ke wajah se mai 1 saal se ghar pe band tha aur ye bhi tha man me ki bachpan me jo sapna engineer ban ne ka dekha tha wo aaj se complete hona shuru hoga college ke liye man me kayi expectation thi. Jaisa ki Karan Johar ki movies me dikhayi jaati hai . But real aur reel me antar hota hai us din meri class me sirf teen log aaye the wo bhi alag alag district se ab marta kya na karta hum teeno saath rahe, kyuki wo dono Allahabad me naye the to maine unhe Allahabad ke baare me kuch bataya unhone bhi mujhe apne home district ke baare me bataya lekin. Fir dean aaye unhone college ka itihaas bataya aur phir hum log ko college dekhne ke liye kaha gya. Lekin college dekhna kya hai mere man me to bas ek cheej thi ki kab mere class me saare bachhe aa jaayenge khair hum log ka 15 days ka induction program chala jis se hua ye ki humari class ke nhi dusre class ke students mere dost ban gye. Ek secret batau jab tak aapki class me koi dost na ho tab tak aapka college me , class me , practical me kahi bhi man nhi lagta . Abhi bhi meri najre us ek bande ko dhud rhi thi jiske saath bakchodi ki jaa sake college final year tak. Lekin yaha to matter palat gya tha sab ladke GIRLFREIND banane me lage , har koi impression jhaad rha hai kuch log seniors se pange le rhe hai meri class me padhai ke alaawa baaki sab ho rha hai. Matlab bakchodi to hum sab mil ke kar rhe the. Lekin alag-alag, phir wo din aaya jab covid ke wajah se college band ho gye ab mujhe koi aisa mil gya tha jo ki bilkul waisa tha jaisa mujhe ek dost me cahahiye tha ab wo kaun hai kaisa hai ye sab bataunga aapko apni next story me tab tak ke liye ghar me rahiye aur UP election ka result dekhte rahiye.
