Hello bhai log mai aapka apna family neighbourhood Abhay aa gya hu apni story ke saath jaise ki aapne meri pichli story College Freind Part 1 me jana tha ki mai college jana start kiya tha aur ek hi mahine me Covid lockdown ke wajah se college band ho gya tha to ab aage


Main padhne me bilkul bhi achha nhi hu isiliye maine apne class ki ek ladki ka number liya kyuki ladke to humse assignment leke likh rhe the aur wo itne smart nhi the ki wo class me dhyaan se padhte ho , Number lene ke baad baate start huyi to phir baate badhne lagi waise galat kahte hai wo log ki ek ladka aur ek ladki kabhi dost nhi ban sakte hai ban sakte hai agar wo khgud pe kaabu rakhe ab hum dono saath me assignment disscuss karte hai , problem solve karte hai , wo humari help kar deti hai mai uski kar deta hu ........ sab kuch badhiya chal rha tha ki mera breakup ho gya ab wo gussa ek din uske kisi joke pe nikal gya mera chuki wo mere se badi thi to wo mera majaak udati rahti thi us din bhi usne mujhe kuch kaha aur maine apni girlfreind ka sara gussa us par utaar diya approx 6 din humari baatein nhi huyi phir ek din maine use msg kiya "Hi, kaisi ho ". Kareeb 10 min me uska reply aaya ki "Chutiye , ye puchne me tujhe 6 din lag gye.". Ek fact batau aapko aapki girlfreind chhod sakti hai, aapki wife aapko divorce de sakti hai but aapki dost jab tak aapki dost hai tab tak aapko nhi chhod sakti hai kyuki Boys freinds matlab ke liye bante hai lekin Girls freind bina kisi matlab ke banti hai . To mera message har us confused engineer ke liye hai ki college jaake girlfreind ke chakkar me padne se jyada achaa rahega ki aap ek freind banao padhayi bhi hogi aur bakchodi bhi, Ye jo love wala factor hai na ye hi insaan ke ant ki shuruwaat hai aur ha jis se bhi dosti karo uske loyal raho phir dost humesha dost honi chahiye kyuki ek baar love wala matter bich me aaya to phir na tum uske lover ban paaogey aur na hi dost . Aur achaa bhi nahi lagega agar tumhari wife tumko kahe ," Chal be chutiye chai bana ke laa." I hope you getting my point . Isee ke saath mai ye chapter yahi close karta hu phir milenge aapse ek nyi story ke saath tab tak ke liye stay safe and alive.
